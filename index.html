01 — Log a reading

Record a reading in seconds

Pick a parameter, type the number, done. TankU shows you the target reef range right next to the field, so you know if you're in trouble before you even save.

02 — Rate how it looks

Not everything shows up on a test strip

Some problems you just notice — a little algae, a coral that's not opening. Log a quick health score alongside your readings, and TankU connects the dots later.

03 — Watch the trend, not just the number

See the story behind the swings

One reading is a data point. A graph is a warning system. TankU lines your parameters up against your health scores so you can see what's actually moving the needle.
